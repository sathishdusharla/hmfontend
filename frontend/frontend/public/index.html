📋 About Our System

Comprehensive Hospital Management

Our Hospital Management System is designed to streamline healthcare operations and improve patient care through digital innovation.

  • Patient Records - Secure digital health records
  • Doctor Schedules - Efficient appointment management
  • Online Appointments - Book from anywhere, anytime
  • Medical Reports - Digital document management
  • AI Analytics - Data-driven healthcare decisions

🧑‍⚕️ Doctor Management

Doctors can efficiently manage patient records, appointments, and update treatment plans in real-time.

📂 Medical Records
